SimplicialComplexes :: chainComplex(SimplicialComplex)

chainComplex(SimplicialComplex) -- The chain complex of boundary maps.

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    chainComplex C
  • Function: chainComplex
  • Inputs:
    • C, a simplicial complex
  • Outputs:
    • a chain complex

Description

The ChainComplex of boundary maps from i-faces to (i-1)-faces.
