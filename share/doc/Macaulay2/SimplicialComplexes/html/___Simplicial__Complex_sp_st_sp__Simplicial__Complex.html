SimplicialComplexes :: SimplicialComplex * SimplicialComplex

SimplicialComplex * SimplicialComplex -- the join of two simplicial complexes

Synopsis

  • Usage:
    J = D * E
  • Operator: *
  • Inputs:
    • D, a simplicial complex
    • E, a simplicial complex
  • Outputs:
    • J, a simplicial complex

Description

Computes join of D and E.

See also joinSimplicial.
